1 How to Pay Your LESCO Bill

You have multiple convenient options to pay your bill, both online and offline.

Online Payment Methods

  • Digital Wallets: Use JazzCash or EasyPasa. Go to 'Bill Payment', select LESCO, enter your reference number, and pay.
  • Bank Apps & Online Banking: Use HBL, UBL, or MCB apps. Navigate to 'Bill Payment', select LESCO, and pay.
  • Raast QR Code: Simply scan the QR code printed on your bill using your bank or wallet app to pay instantly.

Offline Payment Methods

  • Visit any branch of major banks (HBL, UBL, MCB, National Bank) with your bill copy and cash.
  • Pay at any Pakistan Post Office.
  • Visit an authorized retailer (JazzCash or EasyPasa shop).

Note: Online payment is only available until the due date. After that, you must pay offline.

2 Understanding Your LESCO Bill

Key sections of your bill explained:

  • Customer Info & Billing Period: Your details and the dates for which you are charged.
  • Meter Reading: 'Previous' and 'Present' reading used to calculate your consumption in units.
  • Charges Breakdown: Includes cost per unit, meter rent, and Fuel Price Adjustment (FPA).
  • Taxes: Government surcharges like GST, Income Tax, and TV fee.
  • Total Amount & Due Date: The final amount to pay and the last date to avoid late fees.
